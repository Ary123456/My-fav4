My favourite hobbie is cricket. Every Wednesday I go and play cricket and do cricket matches on Saturday. It makes me feel healthy, fit and a stronger person. It makes me feel confident and proud of what I am. I started playing cricket when I was a child of 9 years old.





MY FAVOURITE CRICKET PLAYER




Virat Kohli




Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer. He was born in Delhi, India on November 5, 1988. Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018.
